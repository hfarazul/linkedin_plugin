# Pain points we address

- Hiring even one good engineer takes 2-3 months and burns 30-40% of your seed round
- Most agencies treat you like a brief, not a product partner — generic output, slow iterations
- You're solving the wrong problem precisely because you don't know what's hard to build until you try
- You don't want to learn what a Postgres index is — you want users on a working product
