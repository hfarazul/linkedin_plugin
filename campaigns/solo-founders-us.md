# Pitch

Cortivo AI is an AI-augmented software studio that ships v1 products for non-technical founders. We pair seasoned engineers with AI tooling so you get the velocity of an indie hacker and the quality of a senior team — typically 6-10 weeks from kickoff to a real product in users' hands.

We're built specifically for solo founders who need a partner that can take a Notion doc + a few Loom recordings and turn them into a working app, without you having to spec every screen or learn git.

# Pain points we address

- Hiring even one good engineer takes 2-3 months and burns 60% of your seed round
- Most agencies treat you like a brief — they build what you ask for, not what your users actually need
- You're shipping nothing while competitors with technical co-founders move 5x faster
- You don't want to learn what a Postgres index is — you want a product live

# Proof points

- We typically go from signed engagement to first user testing in week 3
- Embedded model: one founder-engineer pair, not a rotating cast
- AI-first build process keeps eng costs 30-50% below traditional agency rates
- We've shipped products for founders coming from finance, healthcare, retail, and ops

# Tone

Direct and founder-to-founder. No agency-speak. No "we'd love to chat" — short questions that show we read their post or have a specific take. Sound like another solo founder, not a sales rep.
