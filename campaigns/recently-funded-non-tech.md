# Pitch

Cortivo AI is an AI-augmented studio that ships v1s for non-technical founders who've just raised. We pair one senior engineer with AI tooling so you get the velocity of a 3-4 person team for the cost of a fractional senior — typically 6-10 weeks from kickoff to live users.

For a freshly-funded non-tech founder, the math is: spend the next quarter hiring (and probably failing) vs. spend the next quarter shipping. We're built for the second option.

# Pain points we address

- Fully-loaded cost of one senior US engineer is ~25% of a $1M seed in year one — and they take 3 months to even start
- Investors expect velocity that's structurally impossible for a non-tech solo founder
- Most engineering agencies optimize for billable hours; you need product judgement
- Building the wrong thing fast burns more money than building slowly
- Hiring before product-market fit means firing later

# Proof points

- One embedded founder-engineer pair, not a rotating cast — you talk to the same person every week
- AI-first build keeps engineering cost ~40% below traditional studios
- Comfortable with seed-stage chaos: pivots, scope changes, "ship it and see"
- You stay the product owner; we never gate-keep "what's possible"

# Tone

Financial and operational, less emotional. References to burn, runway, hiring math are welcome. Direct — these founders are already getting flooded with sales noise. Sound like an operator, not a vendor.
